Dedicated Environments

Allergen-free and allergen-controlled.
Completely separate.

Most co-packers cannot offer true separation. We can. Our estate includes two physically distinct packing environments — giving food brands the compliance, safety and confidence they need, all on one site.

Allergen-Free Facility
3
Unit · 8,000 sq ft

Allergen-Free Production

Our dedicated allergen-free unit operates under strict protocols to ensure zero cross-contamination risk. This environment is reserved exclusively for products that must be free from all allergens — ideal for brands with sensitive customer bases or strict retailer requirements.

Separate staff, equipment, storage and cleaning regimes ensure this unit remains a fully controlled, allergen-free zone at all times.

  • Physically separate building on the same estate
  • Dedicated staff — no crossover with allergen units
  • Separate equipment, tools and utensils
  • Independent cleaning and sanitation regime
  • Full traceability and documented QC records
  • Ideal for free-from, health food and baby product brands
Allergen-Controlled Facility
4
Unit · 8,000 sq ft

Allergen-Controlled Production

Our dedicated allergen-controlled unit is equipped to handle products containing nuts and other major allergens safely and responsibly. Rigorous management procedures, clear product segregation and thorough cleaning validation mean allergen-containing products are handled with the highest level of care.

This unit operates under documented allergen management procedures fully compliant with BRCGS AA Grade and retailer standards.

  • Dedicated to nut-containing and allergen products
  • Segregated from allergen-free operations at all times
  • Documented allergen management and cleaning procedures
  • BRCGS AA Grade allergen control protocols
  • Full product labelling and traceability
  • Suitable for snacks, confectionery and mixed ingredient products
BRCGS AA Grade certified · Why this matters: Physical separation — not just procedural separation — eliminates cross-contamination risk. Both units share the same estate but operate as completely independent environments with their own staff, equipment and cleaning regimes.
About Complete Packaging Solutions

Leicester's most capable co-packing facility.

We are a specialist contract packing company operating three dedicated production units on the same Leicester industrial estate. With 24,000 sq ft of purpose-built co-packing space across Units 3, 4 and 9 at Ashville Way, we handle everything from high-speed automated lines to meticulous hand-packing.

Critically, we operate two completely separate packing environments — one fully allergen-free facility and one dedicated to allergen-containing products including nuts. This gives food brands the confidence and compliance they need, without compromise.

Our clients range from fast-growing eCommerce brands to major FMCG retailers. One call to CPS is all it takes to get your product packed, labelled and ready for the shelf — on time and on specification, every time.
